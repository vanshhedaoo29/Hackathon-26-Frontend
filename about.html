About the AQI System

Understanding how we monitor and predict air quality

Our Purpose

The AQI Monitor platform aims to empower citizens with real-time air quality information, enabling informed decisions about outdoor activities and health precautions. By combining official monitoring data with AI-powered predictions, we provide a comprehensive view of air quality trends and their potential health impacts.

Key Features

Real-time Monitoring
Live AQI data from monitoring stations across India
AI Predictions
Machine learning models for accurate AQI forecasting
Historical Analysis
Comprehensive trends and pattern recognition
Health Advisories
Personalized recommendations based on AQI levels

AQI Calculation Method

We follow the CPCB (Central Pollution Control Board) standard for AQI calculation, which considers the following pollutants:

PM2.5 - Fine Particulate Matter
PM10 - Coarse Particulate Matter
NO₂ - Nitrogen Dioxide
SO₂ - Sulfur Dioxide
CO - Carbon Monoxide
O₃ - Ozone

AI-Based Prediction

Our AI prediction system uses advanced machine learning models trained on:

Historical AQI patterns and trends
Meteorological data (wind, temperature, humidity)
Traffic and industrial activity patterns
Seasonal and temporal variations
Real-time sensor data integration
